Grade System — BS in Data Science

📐 CGPA Formula
CGPA = Σ(Cᵢ × GP) / ΣCᵢ
Cᵢ = Credits of course i
GP = Grade Points for that course
Σ = Sum over all successfully cleared courses
Pass/Fail courses excluded from CGPA
💡 Grades S to E and P indicate successful course completion. U, W, I are non-passing grades.
